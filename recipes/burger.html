The Perfect Classic Burger

>

Description

Here is a recipe that will always please a hungry crowd. Let's go!

Ingredients:

  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup fine dry crumbs
  1. Preheat grill: preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil grate.
  2. Whisk Ingredients: whisk together egg, salt, and pepper in a medium bowl.
  3. Prepare Beef: add ground beef and bread crumbs in a bowl. Mix them with your hands or fork until they're well blended together.
  4. Make patties: form burger mix into four 3/4 inch thick patties.
  5. Cook patties: place patties on the preheated grill. Cover and cook for about 6-8 minues per side or as to desired doneness. You can measure the temps with a thermometer and make sure burgers read at least 160 degrees F or 70 degrees C.
  6. Serve: enjoy your burgers with your choice of toppings and bread!
